Movie Reviews¶

Reviews play an important role in boosting the success of a film. Lets consider the Internet Revolutionised 20th century. Nowadays when a movie is released,people tend to look at the review ratings of a movie before thinking of booking tickets for the same. So movies with higher review ratings tend to rake in more number of movie goers than a movie which is lowly rated. Thus we take a look into the number of movies and their corresponding review ratings. It is also interesting to find out which movie is highly reviewed and which has a low review rating.

In [19]:
# This function helps in finding out the highest and lowest reviewed movie.
def code(mov):
    #Highest Reviewed Movie In IMDB
    mov=mov.sort_values(by='vote_count',ascending=False)
    vote = mov.iloc[0]
    print('\n Highest Reviewed Movie: \n')
    print(vote)
    #Lowest Reviewed Movie In IMDB
    mov=mov.sort_values(by='vote_count',ascending=True)
    vote_min=mov.iloc[0]
    print('\n Lowest Reviewed Movie: \n')
    print(vote_min)

Conclusions¶

This dataset has been analyzed to find out some critical components to make a movie Successfull. I have analyzed the data by keeping in mind the factors which make a movie to gain success at the BO. The factors considered by me were - Review Ratings, Profit, Cast, Director and Production Houses. I considered review ratings because most of the people nowadays have developed a tendency to watch a movie only after checking out the reviews. So I used it to find the distribution of movies and their corresponding review ratings. I also used runtime to get the review ratings.

Another thing i did was to calculate the profits each movie made. I found out the movies with High and low profit so that i can distinguish easily. Profit was calculated as a measure of budget and revenue.

The final thing was to find the best persons and companies who can deliver a Sucessful film. Thus I created a dataframe which contained records with revenue more than the budget. Thus from this data I was able to derive at a conclusion to which were the best components that can be arranged to make a commercially successfull movie.

Thus considering this factors. A movie with a best director, Cast and a production company will result in a higher amount of profit. The movie delivered will be of a high quality which will eventualy garner higher review ratings.

Limitations¶

Although I have analyzed all the essential factors for a sucessfull movie , I would like to say that this might have its limitations. We can extend this analysis by focusing on factors such as Runtime, Release date and Release year. The evidences I have provided here will not fully guarantee success. While concluding it a famous proverb came to my mind "Too many cooks spoil the broth". Likewise having the best doesnt guarantee that it will end up in the best way only. I would like to say that the analysis I provided will work on numerous occassions but not on all. I would like to add that I only took some factors into consideration and not every available thing.
